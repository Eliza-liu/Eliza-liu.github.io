Video As Art Installation Xiaozhen Liu Title: Microcosmos to World .
Date: March 16th, 2020 .
Medium: Premiere Pro, Music .
Dimension: 1920x1080 .
Description: This is focusing on the Medical Microcosm, I want to turn the whole video's
feeling into a mysterious and melancholic environment by using color bluish-green and blue.
Also, I want to talk about video projection in a glass container to imitate a larger version
of the container to store these microscopic creatures, because in real life these tiny things
can only be seen through a microscope. I found some related videos from the website,
using Premiere Pro to collage them. At the end of the video, I use the earth to show the earth
is created by these wholes. The video is from the beginning of the process of dividing the fertilized egg,
which shows that people grow from the initial cells. Later I showed videos of red blood cells,
white blood cells,and platelets moving in blood vessels, and then the structure of nerve tissue,
which finally constituted the most important part of the entire human body: the heart. These short videos
are used to show that after we grow up, life is composed of different things. Although their size is very
small, they are still important. Behind a video of the human skull formation process, looks very strange.
