Title: Ice Cream Cup
Date: 2020
Medium: Minecraft
Dimension: 2.5cm x 2.5cm
Description: This is an Ice Cream Cup model I made after the first contact with minecraft. I put a biscuit bar on top to make it less symmetrical. The model below is used to test the first print, so the size is very small. Later, I modified the body parts. I cut out the letters to increase the visual effect, but unfortunately the school was closed at that time because of COVID-19. I wish I could print a bigger model if I have the chance in the future.
