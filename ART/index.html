Art Statement


I'm a digital media artist who's interested in Japanese Art, Handicraft
production, and video design. My artworks focus on 3D modeling, internet
culture, and hand-making. Most of my artworks are made by Photoshop Adobe Premiere.
I want to give people the visual experience by using video art, code programming,
and digital illustration.
My video art includes many short video cuts and design, and my original horror
short film. I like the Japanese horror art style such as Curse. I imitated the
Japanese horror theme and recorded original horror short films and design back-view
audio materials. I like to show my audience the emotional feeling.
My hand-making artworks are a lot. It includes 3D artworks and 2D design.
One of my favorite artwork is Temari Ball. It cast me a long time to learn and make.
For 2D design, I made a book that talks about Japanese fairy tales on Fushimi
Inari Taishawith Japanese stab binding. Inside the book are Osaka castle tower,
Japanese flag, fox and cat mask, torii in the lake, Inari god in Japanese mythology,
and Inari god is drawn into a humanoid with a pencil drawing. I made the cover of this
book with cardboard, and then wrapped a layer of watercolor paper on the surface and
painted Torii with orange as the main background color. The purpose of this book is to
express a characteristic stab binding style of Japan, and the content is also a theme
of ancient Japanese mythology. I chose the binding style of Japanese stab binding and
hand-painted my favorite Inari God, Torii and other pictures symbolizing happiness
and peace as the content of the book.
I currently studying at San Jose State University.
I am happy to share my artworks with you!
