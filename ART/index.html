Art Statement

I'm Xiaozhen Liu, you can also call me Eliza. I am a digital media artist who's currently studying at San Jose State University as a senior student. My artworks now are focusing on 3D modeling, internet culture, and Video design. For 2D design, I am familiar with Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop; for 3D modeling, I can use Autodesk Fusion 360 and Blender. By the way, I have two years of study in computer science, therefore, the programming languages I am familiar with are java, part of C#, and simple web programming (HTML). Video design is my favorite part, most of the videos are made by Adobe Premiere and some special effects part is made by Adobe Effect. I want to give people the visual experience by using video art, code programming, and digital illustration. I want to show strong emotion to my audience and give people the visual experience by using video art, code programming, and digital illustration.

When I was in high school, domestic digital media art gradually appeared in people's lives, such as the emergence of many novel interactive interfaces. I also learned about 3D modeling because of watching domestic animation. When I went to university, I often saw some famous artists' works about DMA on Instagram, such as Farid Shukurov and Claudia Hart. The fancy artworks make me very envious and tempted. My major was Computer Science from Spring2018 to Fall 2019. However, I think programming is too simple and boring for me. I love the unrestrained style of art, I hope that my future life can become a part of art, so I decided to switch to Digital Media Art major. During taking the Digital Media Art course last semester, I like ART75's video design. I learned the use of much interesting software, such as Touchdesigner and AE. I think hobbies should be enriched more deeply, rather than just a one-sided understanding; I hope I can learn more in digital media art.

For example, my video art includes many short video cuts and my original horror short film. I like the Japanese horror art style such as Curse. I imitated the Japanese horror theme and recorded original horror short films and design back-view audio materials. Another special effects video made with AE by using the green screen, that also contains a bit of horror, in line with the dark style of Halloween.

I know that my current artwork is not so mature, or there are many areas for improvement. But I will continue to learn and make my work more successful and attractive. I am happy to share my artworks with you!
