Title: Microcosmos to World .
Year: 2020 .
Medium: Premiere Pro, Music .
Dimension: 1920x1080 .
Description: This is focusing on the Medical Microcosm, I want to turn the whole video's
feeling into a mysterious and melancholic environment by using color bluish-green and blue.
Also, I want to talk about video projection in a glass container to imitate a larger version
of the container to store these microscopic creatures, because in real life these tiny things
can only be seen through a microscope. I found some related videos from the website,
using Premiere Pro to collage them. The video is from the beginning of the process of dividing
the fertilized egg, which shows that people grow from the initial cells.

Title: Life in quarantine
By: Xiaozhen Liu
Medium: Adobe Premiere, Photoshop, camera
Dimension: 1920x1080p
Description: For this video, I try to make it have more interesting story line.
It begins with I saw the email sent from school that said campus closed for one week,
and then all classed moved to online. Here I used a piece of music that is very popular
in China to show my happiness. Then is an emotional transition. Since the homework and
pressure, I became very tired. The angels and demon part present my conflicting idea with
living in outbreak of Covid-19. There is an Easter eggs at the end of video.
I want to be able to document some important moments in my life, just like this outbreak.
Although life has become very different, everyone stays at home and work online, it is also
a very valuable memory.
