Title: Magic Seeker
Date: 2019
Medium: Unity
Description: This game is built by Xiaozhen(programmer) and Clarisse(artist) named "Magic Seeker", this is a single-player platform game. I am responsible for making the entire game with Unity. This includes setting up the background and using Tile Palette tool to map of the sketch drawn by the artist in Unity. The movement of characters and the use of sounds.
The goal of this game is for a wizard to collect orbs scattered around the map and go to find the flag to get the final win. Introduction: there will be one level that is completed upon collecting every orb. The objective is to explore and find all 20 orbs. The player can use "A, D" keys to control the direction of the wizard, "A" is to move left, and "D" is to move right. The player can use the "J" key to Jump. The player needs to go through the whole screen to find the blue orbs. After collecting all 20 orbs, the player can go to find the blue flag, after arriving at the flag's position, the player can win this game. There are 20 orbs in total, each orb has 100 scores.
